## Main Scenarios of Use

### Registration

1. Open the application.

![](../img/User%20Guide/start.png)

2. Press the "Register" button.

![](../img/User%20Guide/registration.png)

3. Enter your Name, Surname, Passowrd, Email and Birthdate.
4. Press the button "Registrati!".

[⬆️ Back to Top](#table-of-contents)

### Login

![](../img/User%20Guide/start.png)

1. Enter your Email and your Password used on the [Registration](#registration)

![](../img/User%20Guide/login.png)

2. Press the Log-in button.

![](../img/User%20Guide/welcome.png)

[⬆️ Back to Top](#table-of-contents)

### Edit User Settings

1. Press on "Edit User Settings" after doing the [login](#login).

![](../img/User%20Guide/user%20settings.png)

2. Change whatever you want by pressing the "Change" button of that item.
3. Enter the new value of that item.
4. Press the "Confirm" button to confirm the changes.

![](../img/User%20Guide/edit%20user%20settings.png)

[⬆️ Back to Top](#table-of-contents)

### Navigate Through Elements

1. Press "Start Learning!" after doing the [login](#login).

![](../img/User%20Guide/courses%20view.png)

2. Choose one course from the list.

![](../img/User%20Guide/topics%20view.png)

3. The selected course will be highlighted. Choose one topic from the list.

![](../img/User%20Guide/elements%20view.png)

3. The selected topic will be highlighted. Choose one lesson/quiz from the list.

[⬆️ Back to Top](#table-of-contents)

### Open a Lesson

1. Press on a lesson as in step 3 of [Navigate Through Elements](#navigate-through-elements).

![](../img/User%20Guide/open%20lesson.png)

2. Scroll to view the lesson and eventually press "Torna Alla Home" to go back to the home.

[⬆️ Back to Top](#table-of-contents)

### Complete a Quiz

1. Press on a quiz as in step 3 of [Navigate Through Elements](#navigate-through-elements).

![](../img/User%20Guide/complete%20quiz%201.png)

2. Press "Inizia Quiz" to start the quiz.

![](../img/User%20Guide/complete%20quiz%202.png)

3. Complete the quiz.

![](../img/User%20Guide/complete%20quiz%203.png)

4. Press "Termina Quiz" to finish the quiz and send your replies.

![](../img/User%20Guide/complete%20quiz%204.png)

5. Press "Confirm" after you make sure you replied to everything.
6. Check the results of your submission.

![](../img/User%20Guide/complete%20quiz%205.png)

[⬆️ Back to Top](#table-of-contents)

### Edit a Lesson

1. Right click on a lesson to open the menu.

![](../img/User%20Guide/edit%20lesson%201.png)

2. Press "Modifica" to start editing the lesson.

![](../img/User%20Guide/edit%20lesson%202.png)

3. Use the text editor to write down some content.

![](../img/User%20Guide/edit%20lesson%203.png)

![](../img/User%20Guide/edit%20lesson%204.png)

4. Press "Salva" to save the changes.

5. The lesson will be displayed as in step 1 of [Navigate Through Elements](#navigate-through-elements).

![](../img/User%20Guide/open%20lesson.png)

[⬆️ Back to Top](#table-of-contents)

## Troubleshooting

### Incorrect Login Mail

A message will be displayed in the event an incorrect mail is entered. Try again with proper credentials. If the problem persists, contact the administrator.

![](../img/User%20Guide/incorrect%20login%201.png)


### Incorrect Login Password

A message will be displayed in the event an incorrect password for the selected email is entered. Try again with proper credentials. If the problem persists, try resetting the password or contact the administrator.

![](../img/User%20Guide/incorrect%20login%202.png)
